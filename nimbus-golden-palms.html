A Tropical Sanctuary in Noida

Nimbus The Golden Palms is a lush luxury residential township in Sector 168 — designed to bring the serenity of a tropical resort to your everyday life. The project features palm-lined boulevards, a massive resort-style pool complex, and generous open green spaces.

Large 2, 3, and 4 BHK homes with expansive balconies, earth-toned interiors, and modern fittings make this the preferred address for families seeking space, greenery, and community living without compromising on luxury.
