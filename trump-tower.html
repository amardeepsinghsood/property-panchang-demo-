PROJECT OVERVIEW

Where Prestige Meets Perfection

Trump Tower Noida is a landmark ultra-luxury residential development in Sector 94, set along the Noida Expressway. Developed under the prestigious Trump brand in association with a leading Indian developer, it redefines what premium living means in the NCR region.

The tower rises 56 floors above the Noida skyline, offering breathtaking views of the Yamuna floodplain and the metropolitan horizon. Each residence is meticulously crafted with imported Italian marble, premium German hardware, and bespoke interiors designed to evoke the signature Trump luxury aesthetic.

From the triple-height lobby to the sky lounges and private terraces, every detail speaks of a life lived without compromise.
