Sustainable Luxury for the Future

L&T Green Reserve is a landmark luxury residential project by L&T Realty in Sector 128, Noida. Developed by one of India's most respected conglomerates, this 30-floor project combines sophisticated modern architecture with sustainable building practices.

The project holds a green building certification, featuring solar panels, rainwater harvesting, zero-waste landscaping, and energy-efficient systems — all while delivering the premium interiors and amenities that L&T Realty is renowned for.
