OVERVIEW

The Future of Commercial Real Estate

Bhutani Cyberthum redefines what commercial real estate can be. Located in Sector 140A on the Noida Expressway, this 45-floor architectural marvel houses 1 million sq.ft of premium Grade-A office spaces, luxury retail, and Asia's largest rooftop entertainment zone — including a gaming arena, sky restaurants, and a music performance venue.

The iconic twin towers with a sky bridge, adrenaline rush rides on the rooftop, and a 12-screen multiplex at the podium create an unparalleled commercial ecosystem that attracts thousands of visitors daily.
