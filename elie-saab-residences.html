PROJECT OVERVIEW

Fashion Meets Architecture

Elie Saab Residences is one of India's most audacious luxury real estate collaborations — a project where the vision of world-renowned fashion designer Elie Saab informs every aspect of residential design. Located in Sector 98, Noida, these signature villas are among the rarest addresses in the NCR.

Every villa in this exclusive enclave has been conceptualized with the same attention to detail that defines Elie Saab's haute couture gowns. The result is a home that is both a work of art and a sanctuary of comfort — from custom handcrafted finishes to sculptural staircases and breathtaking landscape gardens.
