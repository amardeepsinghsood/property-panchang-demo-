OVERVIEW

The Art of Living Beautifully

Experion SAATORI in Sector 151 is a masterwork of residential design inspired by Japanese principles of simplicity, harmony, and precision. SAATORI means "the moment of realization" — and every element of this project is designed to create that sublime moment for its residents.

Large 3 and 4 BHK residences with Zen garden courtyards, floor-to-ceiling glazing, premium Japanese-inspired interiors, and carefully curated community amenities make SAATORI a truly one-of-a-kind living experience in NCR.

What Makes SAATORI Extraordinary

01
Zen Design Philosophy
Architecture rooted in Japanese ma (negative space), wabi-sabi, and harmony with nature — creating spaces that calm the mind and uplift the spirit.
02
Japanese Courtyard Gardens
Curated Zen meditation gardens, cherry blossom-inspired landscape, and bamboo groves create space for reflection and tranquility.

Every residence features 9-foot floor-to-ceiling glazing — flooding your home with natural light and offering uninterrupted views of the forest and city.
