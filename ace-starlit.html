PROJECT OVERVIEW

Luxury Living Under a Starlit Sky

ACE Starlit is a signature residential development by ACE Group — one of Noida's most trusted real estate brands. Located in Sector 152, the project offers forest-facing apartments with spectacular views of the Okhla Bird Sanctuary and lush green belts.

The development spans 28 floors and offers 2, 3, and 4 BHK apartments designed for families that seek both luxury and nature's serenity. Premium Italian marble, modular kitchens, and energy-efficient building systems make ACE Starlit a benchmark in modern luxury housing.
